Black Oil Sunflower Microgreens

Organic and easy to grow in soil.

Quantity/Price

6 oz for $9 (enough for two 5" x 10" trays)
12 oz for $16

Highlights

Seed Presoak: 4 hours in cold water
Days to Maturity: 7 - 10 days
Growth Medium: Soil
Flavor: crunchy, nutty, fresh
Nutrients: Vitamins A, B complex, D, and E, calcium, iron, magnesium, potassium, and phosphorus

Instructions

Soak seeds in cold water for 4 hours and plant directly. Spread evenly and tamp very gently to ensure contact with the soil. Mist only as needed. Cover until germination.
